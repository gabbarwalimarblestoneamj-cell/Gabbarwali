How It Works

No Surprises. Just Perfection.

Building a temple is an act of faith. We honor that faith with a transparent, structured process that keeps you involved from the first sketch to the final installation.

01

Consultation & Space Analysis

You reach out to us with your requirements. We discuss the available space in your home or society, the specific deity to be placed, your budget, and any Vastu considerations. For larger projects, we conduct a site visit.

02

AutoCAD Design & Vastu Check

Before touching any stone, our design team drafts a precise AutoCAD blueprint. This ensures exact proportions, correct heights for the deity platform, and full alignment with Vastu Shastra principles. We present this design to you for approval.

03

Stone Selection & Crafting

Once approved, we select the highest grade Ambaji or Makrana marble. Our artisans then begin the meticulous work of cutting, hand-carving the intricate jalis, and shaping the shikharas. We send you photo and video updates throughout this phase.

04

Delivery & Installation

The finished pieces are carefully packed and transported to your location anywhere in Gujarat. Our team arrives on-site to professionally assemble and install the temple, ensuring perfect stability and a flawless finish.
