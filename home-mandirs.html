Bring divine beauty into your home with a hand-carved marble mandir, perfectly sized for your flat, villa, or bungalow.

We understand that every home is unique. That's why we don't just sell pre-made mandirs — we custom craft them to fit your specific dimensions and Vastu requirements.

  • Intricate Jali (Lattice) Work Panels

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

A standard home mandir takes between 15 to 30 days to carve, depending on the complexity of the design and the size.

What marble is used?

We primarily use Ambaji White Marble, directly sourced from our town, and Makrana Marble upon request.

Do you install?

Yes, our team handles delivery and professional installation anywhere across Gujarat.

Can I see a design first?

Absolutely. We provide a precise AutoCAD drawing for your approval before any stone is cut.

What is the price range?

Prices vary based on size and carving details. We offer direct factory pricing, ensuring the best value. Contact us for a precise quote.
